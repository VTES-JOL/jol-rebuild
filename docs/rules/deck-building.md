# Deck Building Rules

Defines how VTES decks are constructed. These rules apply regardless of whether playing on a physical table or online.

---

## Crypt Group Restriction

All crypt cards (except those with group `ANY`) must belong to a single group or two consecutive groups (e.g. groups 2 and 3, or groups 5 and 6). Mixing non-adjacent groups is not allowed. `ANY`-group vampires may be included in any crypt regardless of the groups used.

---

## Format Rules

### Standard Format

- Crypt minimum: 12 cards (no maximum)
- Library: 60–90 cards
- No banned cards

### Duel Format

- Crypt minimum: 6 cards (no maximum)
- Library: 40–60 cards
- Only cards on the app's Duel format eligible card list

### V5 Format

- Same size limits as Standard (crypt min 12, library 60–90)
- No banned cards
- Only cards from the official V5 card list or V5-released sets

---

## Source Notes

- [VEKN Tournament Rules §6.2 Constructed Deck Size Limits](https://archon.vekn.net/document/tournament-rules.html) defines Standard Constructed as 60-90 library cards and a minimum 12-card crypt.
- [VEKN Tournament Rules §4.1.1 Banned List](https://archon.vekn.net/document/tournament-rules.html) defines the official banned list for VEKN tournament play.
- [VEKN Rulebook § Building a Deck](https://www.vekn.net/rulebook) defines the crypt group restriction and the standard crypt/library construction assumptions.
- [VEKN Card Lists](https://www.vekn.net/card-lists) are the source for current card text and official card-list data used by legality checks.
- [VEKN V5 format announcement](https://www.vekn.net/?start=42) describes V5 as an alternate format and lists the included V5 products and promotional cards.
- Duel is treated here as a project-supported format backed by local validator/card-list data. The VEKN tournament rules source above sanctions Standard Constructed, so Duel legality should be checked against the app's configured Duel whitelist rather than inferred from the tournament rules.
